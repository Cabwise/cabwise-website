Privacy Policy

Cabwise · Operated by Bellsle Ltd · Last updated: May 2026

This policy explains how Bellsle Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the Cabwise mobile application and website at cabwise.app.

1. Who we are

Data controller: Bellsle Ltd (United Kingdom).
Contact: support@cabwise.app

2. What Cabwise does

Cabwise is a driver tool that analyses incoming trip offers on your device and displays guidance (for example ACCEPT or DECLINE). Cabwise does not accept or decline trips on your behalf.

3. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and authentication data when you create an account (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Driver settings: your thresholds and preferences (for example £/mile targets, overlay colours), stored on your device and, if you have an active trial or subscription, synced to your account in Firebase Firestore.
  • Subscription status: handled by Google Play Billing; we receive information needed to confirm whether your trial or subscription is active.
  • Support messages: if you email us, we receive the information you choose to send.

4. Trip and screen data

Cabwise processes trip offer information (such as fare and distance) on your device to produce a verdict. This processing is performed to show you the overlay and voice alert in real time.

We do not sell trip data. We do not upload full trip history or a continuous feed of your driver-app screen to our servers for profiling or resale.

To read offer details, Cabwise uses Android accessibility and on-device text recognition while you use supported driver apps. You control this by enabling or disabling permissions in Android Settings.

5. How we use information

  • Provide and maintain the Cabwise app and account features
  • Sync your settings across devices when you sign in
  • Manage trials and subscriptions
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve reliability and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Legal bases (UK GDPR)

Where UK GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the app and subscription), legitimate interests (security and product improvement), and consent where required (for example optional permissions you enable on your device).

7. Sharing

We use service providers who process data on our behalf, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and cloud settings storage)
  • Google Play (subscriptions and payments)

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

8. Retention

We keep account and settings data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward. You may delete your account in the app, which removes associated cloud settings we control.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, and to complain to the UK ICO. Contact us at support@cabwise.app.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Children

Cabwise is not intended for users under 18.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version on this page and update the date above.

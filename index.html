FAQ

Common questions

Does Cabwise accept trips for me?

No. Cabwise only provides guidance — ACCEPT or DECLINE — based on your targets. You still accept or decline offers in your ride-hailing app exactly as you do today.

Which ride-hailing platforms are supported?

Cabwise reads trip offer details displayed on your Android device. Support depends on how offers appear in your platform’s driver app. Contact support@cabwise.app if you need help with your setup.

How does the 3-day trial work?

New subscribers receive a 3-day free trial through Google Play. You will not be charged until the trial ends unless you cancel before it expires. Manage or cancel your subscription in Google Play at any time.

Is my data shared with third parties?

Cabwise is operated by Bellsle Ltd in the United Kingdom. We collect only what is needed to run the service. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data use, retention, and your rights.

Accessibility

Accessibility disclosure

Designed for quick glances

Cabwise uses high-contrast ACCEPT and DECLINE indicators and optional voice announcements so drivers can reduce time looking at detailed fare breakdowns while driving.

Voice announcements can supplement on-screen guidance for users who benefit from auditory feedback. This website aims to meet common accessibility practices: semantic headings, skip navigation, keyboard-focusable controls, and respect for prefers-reduced-motion.

If you encounter barriers using Cabwise or this site, please email support@cabwise.app and we will work to address your needs.
